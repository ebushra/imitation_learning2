CartPole Training Instructions

Goal: Complete each stage by holding the pole within the allowed angle.

Controls: Use Left/Right Arrow keys to move the cart.

Hint: Quickly alternate between the left and right arrow keys. Remember, your actions move the cart, not the pole, so if your pole is leaning to the right, try to push the cart to the right, and vice versa.
