Acrobot Training


Welcome!

This task has two training stages.You will learn how to swing the bottom of the Acrobot to reach a target line.

Below is an example game visual. You are to reach the red line with the bottom joint as fast as possible. The Left / Right arrow keys apply torque to the blue middle joint.

Each stage increases difficulty by increasing the height of the red target line.

Try to reach the line as quickly as possible!

Time: 0.0 s

Goal
Reach the red line with the bottom joint as fast as possible.

Controls
Left / Right arrow keys apply torque to the blue middle joint.

Hint
Pump the swing — go with the motion, not against it.
