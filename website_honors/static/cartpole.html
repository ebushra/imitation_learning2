CartPole Instructions

Goal: Keep the pole balanced upright on the cart for as long as possible. The game will terminate automatically after 250 steps. Complete at least 3 rounds.

Controls
Use the Left and Right Arrow keys to move the cart.

Hint
Quickly alternate between the left and right arrow keys. Remember, your actions move the cart, not the pole, so if your pole is leaning to the right, try to push the cart to the right, and vice versa.
